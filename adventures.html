Adventures

2016 - Appalachian Trail I started hiking on February 22nd and summited Mt. Katahdin on July 4th. Here's a link to my friend Candlemaker's (Tim Bloemendaal) video of our thru-hike. We did a lot of the second half together, so I show up a few times!

2017 - Lowest to Highest Route This route is really cool. It starts at the lowest point in the contintental US (Badwater Basin, -282 ft) and goes to the highest point (14,494 ft). Here's another of Candlemaker's great videos.

2019 - Cross Country Bike Tour I started in San Francisco and biked south to Santa Barbara, then took a left turn and headed across the US to Charleston. It took 52 days, averaging around 68 miles a day.

2020 - Pfiffner Traverse Mile for mile, this is the hardest hike I've done. We followed Andrew Skurka's route, and he sure likes to hike straight off a cliff.

2023 - South America Trip I was on sabbatical in South America from January-July. I started in Ecuador, then went to Chile, Argentina, Uruguay, Colombia, and Peru. I was able to summit my first 20,000 ft mountain (Chimborazo), explore the Amazon, and hike Patagonia's famous W Trek in Torres del Paine National Park.
